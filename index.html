Light Gauge Steel Roof Systems

Light gauge steel roof systems are some of the roof types we specialzie on. These roofs are made of thin, lightweight steel sheets that are cold-formed into structural shapes. They offer a number of advantages, including:

Lightweight

Light gauge steel roof systems are much lighter than traditional wood roof framing, which can result in significant savings on foundation and framing costs

Strong

Light gauge steel is very strong, and can support heavy loads

Durable

Light gauge steel is resistant to fire, rot, and pests, and can last for many years with minimal maintenance
